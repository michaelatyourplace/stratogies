Our Comprehensive Technical SEO Checklist

We perform a deep-dive analysis of every critical element of your site's technical health.

Crawlability & Indexability

We analyze your robots.txt, XML sitemaps, crawl budget, and index status to ensure search engines can efficiently find and rank your content.

Site Speed & Core Web Vitals

We measure your site's performance against Google's Core Web Vitals (LCP, FID, CLS) and provide recommendations to speed things up.

Site Architecture & Linking

We review your URL structure, internal linking, site hierarchy, and click depth to ensure a logical and SEO-friendly structure.

Mobile-Friendliness

With Google's mobile-first index, we meticulously check your responsive design, viewport settings, and mobile usability.

Structured Data & Schema

We check for proper implementation of Schema markup (like FAQ, Product, Local) to enhance your visibility in the SERPs.

Website Security

We verify your HTTPS implementation, check for mixed content issues, and ensure your site is secure for users and search engines.
