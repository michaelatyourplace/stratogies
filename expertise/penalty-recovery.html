How to Know If You've Been Penalized

These are the most common signs of a manual or algorithmic penalty.

Sharp Traffic Drop

A sudden, significant decrease in organic traffic from Google.

Manual Action Notice

A notification of a "manual action" appears in your Google Search Console.

Pages De-Indexed

Key pages of your website have completely disappeared from Google's search results.

Ranking Collapse

Your site no longer ranks for your brand name or your most important keywords.

Our Meticulous Recovery Process

A methodical approach is the only way to successfully recover from a penalty.

Step 1:
Deep-Dive Diagnosis
We use Google Search Console, analytics, and advanced tools to perform a forensic analysis and pinpoint the exact cause of the penalty.
Step 2:
Comprehensive Backlink & Content Audit
We meticulously audit every single backlink and piece of content on your site, classifying them as healthy, low-quality, or toxic.

Step 5:
Monitoring & Future-Proofing
After submission, we monitor your site for signs of recovery and provide recommendations to prevent future penalties.
